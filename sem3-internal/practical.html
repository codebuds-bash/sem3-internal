How It Works

Understanding the Calculation

This calculator computes your practical marks based on the following components:

Calculation Formula

  • Experiments (10): 30 marks each, scaled to 45 marks total
  • Lab MST: 15 marks maximum
  • Final Practical: 40 marks maximum

The calculator sums all these components to give you a final practical marks score out of 100.

Formula

Total = (Sum of Experiments / 300) × 45 + Lab MST + Final Practical
