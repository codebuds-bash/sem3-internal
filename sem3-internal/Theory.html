How It Works

Understanding the Calculation

This calculator computes your internal marks based on the following components:

Calculation Formula

  • Assignment: Direct marks (out of 10)
  • Attendance: Direct marks (out of 2)
  • MST 1 & 2: Average of both MSTs (out of 20 each)
  • Quiz: Direct marks (out of 4)
  • Surprise Test: Weighted calculation (12 → 4 marks)

The calculator sums all these components to give you a final internal marks score.

Formula

Total = Assignment + Attendance + (MST1 + MST2)/2 + Quiz + (Surprise Test / 12) × 4
